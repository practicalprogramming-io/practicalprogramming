We believe that...

With a little bit of patience and a few drops of sweat, anyone can teach themself how to write computer code.

We would know, after all, most of the content on practicalprogramming.io is written by self-taught software engineers.  One of greatest aspects of this industry and computer programming in general is how accessible it is.  Whether you're looking to learn some basic scripting to automate a repetitive workflow or you're seeking to pursue a career in software engineering, the only barrier to entry is a computer and an internet connection!

Having mentored people from a variety of different careers and walks of life who want to break into the world of computer code, we're aware of the bombardment of educational options available.  Most of these options are more than happy to take very large amounts of money from their students - code "boot camps" and online courses that cost thousands of dollars.

Enrolling in code "boot camps" and expensive online courses is becoming a popular trend amongst people looking to break into the industry.  We at practicalprogramming.io promise you that there is nothing you'll learn at one of these institutions that you can't learn for free on the internet.   Before enrolling with a "boot camp" or online course, give practicalprogramming.io a shot!
